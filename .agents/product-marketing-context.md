# Product Marketing Context

Last updated: 2026-03-30

## Product Overview

- **One-liner:** Personal portfolio and online resume of Frank Blechschmidt, Senior Engineering Manager.
- **What it does:** Showcases Frank's professional experience, projects, patents, and skills. Serves as the canonical online presence for recruiters, hiring managers, and professional peers. Provides machine-readable endpoints (JSON Resume, LLMs.txt, MCP, Skills) for AI agents and search engines.
- **Product category:** Personal portfolio / online CV
- **Product type:** Static site (Astro SSR on Cloudflare Workers)
- **Business model:** N/A (personal brand)

## Target Audience

Primary audiences:

- Recruiters and talent sourcers searching for senior engineering leaders
- Hiring managers evaluating candidates for EM/director-level platform and infrastructure roles
- Professional peers and collaborators in cloud infrastructure / developer tools
- AI agents and search engines indexing professional profiles

Primary use case: Evaluate Frank's background, experience, and technical depth for leadership roles or professional collaboration.

Jobs to be done:

- Quickly assess qualifications and career trajectory
- Download a print-ready resume for internal sharing
- Reach out via email or LinkedIn
- Verify technical depth and innovation track record (patents, projects)

## Problems and Pain Points

Core problem: Engineering leaders' online presence is often fragmented across LinkedIn, GitHub, and outdated personal sites — making it hard for recruiters and peers to get a complete picture.

Why alternatives fall short:

- LinkedIn profiles are templated and lack personality or technical depth
- GitHub profiles don't convey leadership experience
- Most personal sites are stale or purely decorative

What it costs them: Missed opportunities, incomplete candidate evaluation, extra research time.

## Differentiation

Key differentiators:

- AI-native site with MCP server, LLMs.txt, JSON Resume, and structured schema — signals cutting-edge technical awareness
- Machine-readable formats alongside human-readable content
- Patent holder (signals depth of innovation)
- Single source of truth: resume data drives all formats (PDF, JSON, HTML, LLM-friendly text)

Why that matters: Demonstrates the same product-minded engineering approach Frank brings to teams — building systems that serve multiple consumers from a single source of truth.
